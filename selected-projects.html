Software & AI Projects
ChatterBot — Multi-Agent Document Intelligence
2025

Originated as an exploration of NVIDIA's CUDA-Q quantum computing library; evolved into a production document intelligence platform. Accepts arbitrary combinations of web URLs (with automatic sub-page crawling) and uploaded documents, indexing all content into a local vector database. The core architectural departure from standard retrieval-augmented generation: multiple independent agents query the vector store in parallel via a hierarchical data structure, surfacing distinct evidence fragments before a synthesis model constructs the final response.

DepthPro — Metric 3D Reconstruction from Single Images
2024

Extended Apple Research's monocular depth model (DepthPro) beyond the relative-depth visualizations available in existing public demos. Forked and modified the HuggingFace Space to expose the model's native metric output — real physical depth in meters — and added an export pipeline generating downloadable textured OBJ files from any single photograph. Drawing on direct experience shipping Face ID depth-sensing hardware at iPhone scale, the goal was to surface the full capability of the underlying model rather than its cosmetic output.

AI Video Hero Mode — Cinematic AI Generation
2024

Explored the frontier of AI video generation by scripting fully realized cinematic sequences — complete with dialogue, camera direction, and visual effects — rendered via Seedance 2.0. Produced three end-to-end videos, then developed an automated harness for script generation at scale. Key finding: fully autonomous video generation from arbitrary prompts remains one model release away; deliberate human direction at the scripting and prompt level is still the differentiating factor between commodity output and compelling content.

Hardware & Physical Projects
LED Motion-Reactive Wearable Suit
2017

A wearable light system in which LED output is entirely driven by the wearer's motion: acceleration magnitude controls brightness while X/Y/Z axes map across the full RGB color spectrum, producing a unique color signature for every direction of movement. IMU sensors at the hands and feet feed dual Arduino microcontrollers mounted on a custom back harness. Premiered in a live demonstration with professional dancer Allie Truax. The design challenge was building a harness sufficiently lightweight and quick-donning that performers with no technical background could wear it without restriction.

FPV Drone — Caddx GoFilm 20 Cinema Build
2025

Built and piloted a Caddx GoFilm 20 — a 4K cinema-grade FPV quadcopter optimized for smooth, stabilized aerial cinematography rather than freestyle aerobatics. The project validated a personal hypothesis: under $1,000 in hardware and under 100 hours of flight practice are sufficient to access a mode of aerial filmmaking previously reserved for professional operators. All footage is original, shot over the builder's property.

Of Animals and Art — Children's Book Series
2024

A published two-volume children's book series presenting the same animal subjects rendered across distinct historical art movements — photorealism, Art Deco, Impressionism, and others — side by side. The pedagogical premise: children absorb visual vocabulary (artistic style, period, technique) through direct exposure before formal explanation, in the same way they acquire language. AI image generation made it practical to commission the same subject across dozens of styles at the quality level required for print publication.
